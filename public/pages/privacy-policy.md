# Privacy Policy for LiaoTian
### Effective Date: November 10, 2025
This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed when using LiaoTian (the "Service").

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

## 1. Information We Collect

- Account Data: Email, username, password (hashed), profile information (bio, avatar, etc.).
- User Content: Posts, messages, uploaded files (images, videos, documents, etc.), group chats, calls, metadata.
- Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device info, timestamps, interactions (likes, follows, views).
- Authentication Data: Managed via Supabase Auth (email/password or third-party if enabled).

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the Service (e.g., display feeds, deliver messages).
- To enable features like following, groups, and real-time updates.
- To moderate content and enforce Terms.
- To improve the Service (analytics, debugging).
- To communicate (e.g., notifications, verification requests).

## 3. Data Storage and Security
- Data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL database).
- We use reasonable measures to protect data, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest where provided by Supabase.
- Passwords are hashed; we never store plain-text passwords.

## 4. Sharing Your Information
- Publicly: Posts, profiles, and follows are visible based on your settings and instance configuration (e.g., all posts or follow-only feed).
- With Others: Messages and group content are shared with recipients.
- Service Providers: Supabase (database/auth) and Vercel (hosting) process data as subprocessors.
- Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect rights/safety.

We do not sell your personal data.

## 5. Data Retention
We retain your data as long as your account is active or needed for the Service. Deleted content may be retained in backups for up to 30 days.

## 6. Your Rights
- Access, correct, or delete your data via user settings.
- Request export or deletion by contacting us.
- Opt out of non-essential communications.

## 7. Third-Party Links and Embeddings
The Service may contain links or embeds to external sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

## 8. Children's Privacy
We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If discovered, we will delete it.

## 9. International Transfers
Data is stored in Supabase regions (default: US East). Transfers occur as needed for the Service.

## 10. Changes to Policy
We may update this Policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.

## 11. Contact
For privacy questions, use the Service's support or contact the instance operator.
